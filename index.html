Typey Bush

Developed in Godot
Total Dev Time: ~3 months

This is the first game I ever made! You'll notice this took a long time to make for what it is... please excuse younger me's inexperience.
This is a "Flappy-like" with 2 main differences: it features 43rd President George W. Bush, AND rather than just hitting the spacebar, you have to type out wonderful George Bush quotes that are presented to you.

PLEASE NOTE: This is not anti or pro George Bush media. I think we can all agree he's said many funny things. I simply wish to bring these words of wisdom to more people.
Also, I hope the presence of noncontroversial, Do Nothing Ike offsets any understandable feelings people have when they see George Dubyah.

As of 8/12/25, Typey Bush has 281 views on Newgrounds.com, and 37 on Itch.io

Dolomite Joe

Developed in Godot
Total Dev Time: 1 Week

Dolomite Joe was created for the University at Buffalo Game Development Club's 2024 Halloween Game Jam.
The theme was "automation", and one of the bonus themes was "rocks". From this, my narrative masterwork was created

Dolomite Joe sees a local family rock farmer (named Dolomite Joe) fighting against the takeover of his farmland from a malevolent force with a "Synthetic Rock Machine", threatening his family's business as well as the rock farming industry as a whole.
The core gameplay involves text based "negotation" sections with various prominent members of the rock industry. This involves a classic "pick the right conversation options" section, a game where you must tell the difference between real and synthetic rocks, and a historical trivia section!

As of 8/13/25, Dolomite Joe has 220 views on Newgrounds.com, and 31 on Itch.io
